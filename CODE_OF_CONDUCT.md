# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

claude-lens is an open-source project that welcomes contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels. We are committed to maintaining a respectful, collaborative, and productive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming and considerate in your communication
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus discussions on the technical merits of ideas
- Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or derogatory comments directed at other contributors
- Public or private conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting
- Sustained disruption of discussions or deliberate obstruction of contributions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where contributors represent the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a [GitHub private security advisory](https://github.com/Pixerova/claude-lens/security/advisories/new) or contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions or comments that violate this policy, and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors who engage in sustained unacceptable behavior.
